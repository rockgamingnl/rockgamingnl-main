About Us

Who We Are

Rock Gaming NL is Newfoundland & Labrador's first dedicated gaming community hub. We're built by gamers, for gamers.

Our mission is to connect players across the province, showcase local streaming talent, and put NL on the competitive gaming map. Whether you're a casual player, aspiring streamer, or esports enthusiast, you've got a home here on the Rock.
